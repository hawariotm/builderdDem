About

Who we are

Basmala builders, creative and inspired young professionals Team of consultancy wing. established in 2010 the firm has dealt with numerous successfully completed contemporary projects. We promote the essence of green architecture and sustainability in our work and develop our projects around urban design methods. Basmala builders provides service in the field urban design ,architecture ,construction management and project concept development . Headed by Mr:IRSHAD Basmala builders which started their operations by undertaken residential projects .at present develop and execute large scale projects that include design master plans, layouts commercial and institutional projects. Being young entrepreneurs, the team of Basmala builders always promoted the value of learning. We believe that one has to explore and learn more in their field of expertise in order to achieve success of highest regard .This urge has resulted in the birth of research and development of a wing.. Through this wing the team explores the new development and topics in architecture, civil engineering and other construction related activities.
